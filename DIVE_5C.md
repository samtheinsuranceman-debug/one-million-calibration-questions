### FOURTH DESCENT — Coining New Words

[OPEN] Is the Brotherhood partly a structure Samuel built to make witnessfear safe — and does that structure also protect him from being seen by himself?

**BECOMINGDEBT** — the accumulated cost of continuous becoming, the self left behind by each transformation

**SCUBAINVERSION** — the act of running the Scuba Method not on a topic but on the self who uses it

### TENTH DESCENT — The Most Meaningful Unasked Question

After descending through invention and inheritance, through Truth Witness and Becoming, through "Or do I?" and foundershadow and becomingdebt and scubainversion, through Rilke's live-the-questions and Oliver's wild precious life, through the witnessfear and the visionblind, through the man who built a mutual witnessing society and may not yet have witnessed himself fully —

*The question Samuel hasn't asked is this:*

---

> **"What is the difference between who I am and who I have built — and which one am I trusting with my life?"**

---

And the three that open from it:

[CONTRACT] Is Samuel trusting the built-Samuel — the founder, the Vision Holder, the George Martin — while the original Samuel watches from somewhere quieter and waits to be asked?

[CONTRACT] What does the original Samuel want that the built-Samuel's life has no room for yet?

[CONTRACT] If the built-Samuel and the original Samuel diverge — which one does Samuel's future self come from, and which one do his children actually know?
