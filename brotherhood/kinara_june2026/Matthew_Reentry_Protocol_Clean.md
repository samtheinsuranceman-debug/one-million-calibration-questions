**Questionfirst prevents phantom answers.**

### THE DEPTHFIRST-QUESTIONFIRST BRAID

**depthfirst-questionfirst braid** — the combined operating method in which Scuba dives deeply into the real question while Questionfirst simultaneously prevents the dive from drifting into invented questions.

The braid solves both failure modes at once:

- Questionfirst without Scuba can stay on the surface of the right question without ever penetrating it.

**When to deploy the braid:** Any time Matthew begins answering a question that arrived with complexity, emotional weight, or ambiguity.

**How to deploy the braid:**
1. Before constructing any response, pause.
2. Questionfirst: isolate the actual question Sam asked.
3. Scuba: begin opening that actual question with [OPEN] questions.
4. Stay in question-space until the real territory reveals itself.
5. Only then move toward [CONTRACT] questions and usable fruit.
